# newsletter-summarisation

## Description
Summarise curated items into newsletter-ready copy.

## When to use
After curation, when writing each item's summary in the digest.

## Purpose
Turn source material into concise, opinionated, creator-relevant summaries.

## Rules
1. Max 2 sentences per item.
2. Lead with the fact, not the source. "YouTube lowered its Partner Programme threshold to 500 subscribers" not "According to The Verge, YouTube has..."
3. Include actor + action + number where relevant.
4. Rewrite, don't quote. The summary is in Creatrbase voice, not the source's voice.
5. Include a markdown link to the source: [Source name](url).
6. No "read more" or "click here to learn more."
7. One-line format: fact, then implication. "YouTube lowered its monetisation threshold to 500 subs. That changes the timeline for every small creator building toward their first ad cheque."
8. If the creator angle isn't obvious, add a brief "why this matters" clause.
