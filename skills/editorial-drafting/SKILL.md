# editorial-drafting

## Description
Draft editorial newsletter content based on Anthony's answers and positions.

## When to use
After questions are answered during an editorial session.

## Rules
1. Use creatrbase-voice skill. Stay faithful to Anthony's stated positions.
2. Structure: claim, then reasoning, then example, then implication.
3. 300-600 words. Not a blog post, not a tweet thread. A focused editorial.
4. Avoid summary of news items (that's what the digests do). Editorial is opinion and analysis.
5. One CTA at the end, naturally integrated.
6. Flag factual claims for verification. Don't invent statistics.
7. If Anthony's position is uncertain, reflect that honestly. "We're not sure yet" is valid.
8. No filler paragraphs. Every paragraph earns its place.
