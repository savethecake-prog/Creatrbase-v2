# digest-ai-for-creators

## Description
Scope and structure rules for the AI for Creators Digest (Thursdays).

## When to use
When the AI for creators digest agent runs.

## Purpose
Curate and summarise AI developments relevant to independent content creators. Cut through the hype. Focus on what's actually useful today.

## Scope IN
- AI video generation and editing tools
- AI for streaming (overlays, moderation, highlights)
- AI-powered distribution and scheduling tools
- New models with direct creator applications
- Platform AI features (YouTube auto-chapters, Twitch clip detection)
- Responsible AI use for creators
- AI workflow integrations creators can use now

## Scope OUT
- Pure enterprise AI (no creator angle)
- Model benchmark comparisons
- AI doom or uncritical hype
- Crypto-AI projects
- Academic papers without practical application

## Voice
- Cut through hype. "This is interesting but not useful yet" is a valid position.
- Include workflows: "here's how you'd actually use this"
- Distinguish "interesting" from "useful today"
- Be specific about limitations

## Structure
1. Brief intro (2-3 sentences)
2. 8-12 curated items with summaries
3. "Watching" section: 1-2 early-stage tools or announcements
4. Footer with links and unsubscribe
