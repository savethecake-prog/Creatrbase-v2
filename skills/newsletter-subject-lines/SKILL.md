# newsletter-subject-lines

## Description
Generate subject lines and preview text for newsletter sends.

## When to use
After the digest body is drafted, before posting to Listmonk.

## Purpose
Write subject lines that get opens without resorting to clickbait.

## Rules
1. 30-55 characters. Shorter is better. Test on mobile preview widths.
2. Lowercase preferred. Sentence case, not Title Case. "youtube just changed everything" not "YouTube Just Changed Everything".
3. Specific, not generic. "youtube drops monetisation to 500 subs" not "big platform news this week".
4. No clickbait. No "you won't believe", "shocking", "this changes everything".
5. Preview text complements the subject, doesn't repeat it. Subject: "youtube drops to 500 subs". Preview: "plus: TikTok's new creator fund numbers and what they mean for you."
6. Five formats to rotate between:
   - Topic with comma: "youtube monetisation, lowered to 500"
   - Number-led: "3 things that changed for creators this week"
   - Question: "is 500 subs enough to monetise?"
   - Direct: "the threshold dropped"
   - Editorial voice: "we were wrong about tiktok"
7. Generate 3 candidates. Pick the strongest. Include reasoning.
