# digest-creator-economy

## Description
Scope and structure rules for the Creator Economy Digest (Mondays).

## When to use
When the creator economy digest agent runs.

## Purpose
Curate and summarise the week's most important creator economy news for independent creators with 1k-100k subscribers, UK and US focused.

## Scope IN
- Platform changes affecting creators (YouTube, Twitch, TikTok, Instagram)
- Brand deal market shifts, agency news, rate changes
- Legal and regulatory changes affecting creators
- Creator partnerships and collaborations
- Research and data about the creator economy
- Tools and services for independent creators

## Scope OUT
- Pure AI news (that's the other digest)
- Entertainment industry gossip
- Agency-side marketing news
- Stories only relevant to 1M+ creators
- TikTok-exclusive stories (limited API, can't score)
- Crypto/NFT/Web3 creator projects

## Structure
1. Brief intro (2-3 sentences, sets the tone)
2. 8-12 curated items with summaries
3. "Watching" section: 1-2 developing stories not ready for a full item
4. Footer with links and unsubscribe
